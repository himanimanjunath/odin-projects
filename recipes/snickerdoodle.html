Snickerdoodles

Description

This easy snickerdoodle recipe that everyone loves is from someone's great-grandma! The cookies are soft and full of cinnamon flavor.

Ingredients

  • White Sugar
  • Softened Butter
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Cream of Tartar
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Ground Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven
  2. Mix cookies
  3. Make coating
  4. Form dough into balls
  5. Bake cookies
